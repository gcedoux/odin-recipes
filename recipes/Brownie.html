Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Water
  • Powder sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Mix all liquids in a bowl
  2. Add the dry Ingredients and mix
  3. Put in a pan and bake at 350-degrees for 30 minutes
  4. let cool for 10 minutes and enjoy!
